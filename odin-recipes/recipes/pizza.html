Pizza

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the sauce:

  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the toppings:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes or until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, olive oil, salt, and yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes or until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the dough to a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  8. In a small bowl, combine the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Spread the sauce over the dough.
  9. Top the pizza with the mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, and bell peppers.
  10. Bake the pizza for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
